### üå± Getting Started

- **Connect device:** Click on `Connect` to select your device.
- **Experiments:** Select your desired experiment type *(e.g. Bending, Tension, ect.)*, enter input details *(e.g. Loading distance, Stopping force, ect.)*, and press `Start Experiment` button.
- **Emergency:** Press `Stop Machine` button in case of emergency or press `Emergency` button on the machine.
- **Monitor Data:** Use the dashboard to track live updates, graphs, or logs in real-time.
- **Execute Commands:** Select functions from the menu to send specific commands.
- **Export Results:** Export results with the `Export` button to save output as *CSV*, *XLSX*.

#### üí° Tips

- For optimal performance, use Chromium-based browsers, such as *Chrome* or *Edge*.
- Make sure your device is powered and connected before using the app.
