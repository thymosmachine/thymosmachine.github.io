### Getting Started

- **Connect device:** Click on `Connect` to select your device.
- **Monitor Data:** Use the dashboard to track live updates, graphs, or logs in real-time.
- **Execute Commands:** Select functions from the menu to send specific commands.
- **Export Results:** Export results with the `Export` button to save output as *CSV*, *XLSX*, *JSON* or image.
